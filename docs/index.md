# Clear Ledger

**Simple Bill & Tax Tracker**

Clear Ledger is a privacy-first Android app for tracking bills, invoices, and tax-related records locally on your device. Clear Ledger does not send your data to the developer or operate its own cloud service. Android system backup may apply depending on your device settings.

## Privacy-first

Clear Ledger does not require an account, does not use a backend server, and does not send your data to the developer. Manual export and backup files are created only when you choose to save them. If Android system backup is enabled, Android/Google may back up app data per your device settings.
