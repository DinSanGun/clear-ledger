# Clear Ledger

**Simple Bill & Tax Tracker**

Clear Ledger is a privacy-first Android app for tracking bills, invoices, and tax-related records locally on your device.

## Links

- [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy) — hosted at `https://dinsangun.github.io/clear-ledger/privacy-policy`
- [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/DinSanGun/clear-ledger)

## Privacy-first

Clear Ledger does not require an account, does not use a backend server, and does not collect your data. Export and backup files are created only when you choose to save them.
